Introducing My Cozy Life

My journey to make my surrounding life warm and cozy started with my favorite content creator - Maisy Leigh. I have been following her for upwards of 2 years. Being apart of her discord community has brought me lots of joy and happiness! Maisy has a design studio that she runs with her family - together they did a lighting collaboration with Gantri. I would love to own one of those lamps but they are VERY expensive! Click here to check out the collab!

Desk Highlights

  • Peg Board: having this gives me the freedom to be creative with my nick nacks. Sometimes I struggle with the balance of decorating it vs having room to keep it functional!
  • Monitor Riser: the monitor riser I really want is ~$250~ I wasn't able to find one to fit what I had in mind exactly - so I built my own!
  • Lighting: There are a lot of different thing at my desk that light up and provide some ambient lighting. The rabbit, cat, and toast all light up!
  • Art: I have many different art pieces surrounding my desk. You can't see all of them in the photo. There are 5 Studio Ghibli prints and one Lord of the Rings print. See if you can spot them in my desk photo!
  • Keyboards: I have found a great way to display my keyboards so I am able to rotate them in and out each day
